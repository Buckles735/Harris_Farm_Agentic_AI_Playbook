# Harris Farm Markets — Agentic AI Playbook

## Overview

This repository contains the Agentic AI Playbook for Harris Farm Markets, identifying the **top 10 automation opportunities** where agentic AI can transform operations across the business.

## Contents

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `Harris_Farm_Agentic_AI_Playbook.pptx` | PowerPoint deck — 17 slides covering all 10 opportunities, roadmap, and ROI |
| `Harris_Farm_Agentic_AI_Playbook.html` | Web version of the playbook — fully responsive, single-file, no dependencies |
| `Harris_Farm_Workflow_Agent_Prototype.html` | Interactive prototype of the Internal Workflow Agent with live dashboard, chat interface, and agent views |

## Top 10 Agentic AI Opportunities

1. **Smart Demand Forecasting** — Auto-generate purchase orders using weather, events, and sales patterns
2. **Waste Reduction & Markdown** — Predict shelf life, auto-trigger markdowns, route to charity/Imperfect Picks
3. **Workforce Scheduling** — Optimal rosters based on traffic forecasts, skills, and award compliance
4. **Supplier Management** — Auto-negotiate, coordinate POs, flag quality issues across 247+ suppliers
5. **Customer Experience** — Handle inquiries, personalise recommendations, manage loyalty engagement
6. **Dynamic Pricing** — Monitor competitors and auto-adjust prices within guardrails
7. **Quality & Compliance** — Continuous IoT monitoring, auto-audits, certification tracking
8. **Store Operations** — Auto-assign tasks, monitor completion, coordinate HQ directives
9. **Amazon & Online Fulfilment** — Optimise pick paths, manage capacity, predict bottlenecks
10. **Data & Decision Intelligence** — Natural-language business queries, automated leadership briefings

## Key Numbers

- **$15-25M** estimated annual savings
- **40%** time reclaimed for strategic work
- **12-18 months** full deployment timeline
- **718%** projected 3-year ROI
